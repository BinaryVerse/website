## **Digital Twin Platform Demo: Process Industry Simulator**

### **Overview**

This platform is a high-fidelity **Digital Twin and Virtual Experimentation Environment** tailored for the chemical, process, and manufacturing industries. It seamlessly bridges chemistry database management, physical equipment configuration, and advanced Machine Learning (ML) to simulate, test, and optimize chemical processes virtually.

---

### **Key Features & Walkthrough Modules**

#### **1. Comprehensive Chemical Intelligence Database**

* **Centralized Inventory:** Includes a detailed library of elements and compounds (e.g., Hydrogen, Styrene, Solvents, and Acids) mapped with their standard CAS numbers and chemical formulas.
* **Molecular Visualization:** Provides seamless switching between **2D chemical structures** and interactive **3D molecular models**.
* **Comprehensive Data Profiles:** Captures critical data including SMILES notations, precise physical properties (boiling/melting points, molecular weight, state at room temperature), and visual characteristics.

#### **2. Smart Equipment Configuration & Physics Modeling**

* **Industrial Asset Library:** Pre-configured with industry-standard equipment nodes such as Centrifugal Pumps, Distillation Columns, Batch Reactors, Continuous Stirred-Tank Reactors (CSTR), and Heat Exchangers.
* **Hybrid Modeling Capability:** Allows engineers to embed custom Python script code for core physical/thermodynamic equations alongside incoming data streams.
* **Flexible Dynamic Mapping:** Lets users fully customize assets by defining variable constraints, targeted outcomes (e.g., energy consumption, pressure drop, flow rate), operating parameters, and multi-directional stream logic (such as configuring suction and discharge flow rates).

#### **3. No-Code Data-Driven AI Training**

* **Template-Driven Workflows:** Users can download pre-structured CSV datasets tailored to the exact parameters of the configured equipment.
* **Automated Data Validation:** Uploaded production or synthetic data is instantly parsed and validated by the platform.
* **Data Distribution Visualization:** Includes built-in analytical charts that plot historical variables (e.g., operational temperatures, pump heads, and pressures) to give a transparent overview of training data.
* **One-Click ML Modeling:** Features a low-code "Train Model" pipeline where users build ML-driven predictive models directly inside the asset's digital twin interface.

#### **4. Drag-and-Drop Virtual Experimentation Canvas**

* **Node-Based Process Flow Diagram (PFD):** Users can map an entire production plant floor visually by dragging and dropping smart assets onto an expansive virtual canvas.
* **Interconnected Pipeline Simulation:** Enables the seamless chaining of multiple equipment nodes—such as connecting the discharge outcome of one centrifugal pump straight into the suction stream of the next.
* **Cross-Node Parameter Mapping:** Allows engineers to define complex real-world dependencies, mapping dynamic outputs like product flow rates and energy drains across an entire sequential system loop.
